Structure

Headers

  • \n(\n)<=6 words\n
  • A line followed up by a bunch of "---" or "___" or "==="

New Line

  • \n => <br />

Paragraphs

  • \n\n => <p>...</p>

Quotes

  • One or more sentences surrounded with ' or "
  • Sentence after a ":"

Blockquotes

  • A paragraph sorrounded with ' or "

Horizontal rules

  • more than one "-" or "_" for example ---- or ____

Lists

  • An unordered List begins with * or -
  • An ordered Lists with 1/2/3 or 1./2./3.
